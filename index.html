About

As a florist, I was stung by the developer bug while in pursuit of building a website for ‘Sweetpea’ a business I have setup and run over the past 10 years. Applying my background in graphic design I created the site and set out to acquire the development skills needed to bring the project to life.

On completing General Assembly’s front-end development course and creating ‘The Sweetpea Florist’ website, which lead to a 40% increase in sales over the following three months, I felt a blossoming passion to code. I further pursued this by completing General Assembly's JavaScript course, and implemented all my knowledge into creating 3 more websites.

I am currently looking for the opportunity to join a dynamic team of developers, excited about all things front-end with an interest in diversity, adopting practices such as pair programming, user-focused design, test driven development, and continuous improvement.
